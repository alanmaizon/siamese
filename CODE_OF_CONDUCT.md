# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
- Be respectful, clear, and professional.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Give actionable feedback tied to code and behavior.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors who violate this policy.
